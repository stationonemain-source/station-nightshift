03 What we found

Website is a Canva subdomain

Google lists mntlbarbellclub.my.canva.site as the official site. Canva subdomains rank poorly in local SEO and signal low credibility to prospects researching before committing to a gym membership.

Phone number mismatch

The intake form lists 281-814-3500 but Google's verified profile shows 979-307-9630. Two different numbers in circulation means calls and texts are almost certainly being split or lost entirely.

No confirmable web content

Pricing, membership tiers, class schedules, and coaching bios are unverifiable online. Prospects who can't self-qualify before visiting will drop off before ever contacting you.

Strong reviews, weak digital foundation

A 4.9-star rating across 44 Google reviews is exceptional social proof, but with no indexable website behind it, that trust signal has nowhere to convert into new memberships.
